Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working together.

How quickly can you start my project?

I typically respond to inquiries within 24 hours. Once we agree on project scope and timeline, I can usually start within 1-2 weeks, depending on my current workload and project complexity.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! I offer a free initial consultation to understand your project requirements, discuss technical approaches, and provide you with a detailed proposal and timeline.

What's your development process?

I follow a 4-step process: Discovery (understanding requirements), Planning (technical architecture), Development (agile development with regular updates), and Deployment (production launch with monitoring).

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely! I provide ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your application stays secure, updated, and performs optimally. Support packages can be customized based on your needs.

Can you work with my existing team?

Yes, I'm experienced in collaborating with existing development teams, designers, and project managers. I can integrate seamlessly into your workflow and communication tools.
